About Split Bills Fairly

Food, Friends, and Figuring out who owes what. The basis for creating this calculator. Throughout our years, "splitting the bill" has meant different things: splitting a Happy Meal, sharing a lane at the bowling alley, splitting a beer run, or settling up a night out. As our friends, lifestyles, and habits change - one thing remained the same: figuring out who owes what and ensuring everyone is paid fairly.

That's what this calculator was built for. Our goal is to create something helpful and "in the moment" (quick) enough that it's immediately useful.

Our professional jobs have been in website creation and marketing, and we wanted to combine the 3 into 1: love of group experiences + values in helping others + professional skills = this website.

A part of the ads for Good network

This tool is connected to ads for Good, a mission-driven company focused on education about the advertising industry and focusing on people over profits.
